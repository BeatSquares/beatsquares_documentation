## Quick Start

Once we have setup your account in BeatSquares, you can create new squares and generate new formats straight away.

## What You Can Do

- **Squares**: Define topics and target audiences for tailored content selection
- **Podcasts**: Select articles, generate a script and create a finished podcast  
- **Newsletters**: Use your content to automatically generate newsletters
- **WhatsApp Messages**: Generate WhatsApp messages based on articles and various online sources
