The dashboard is your starting point in BeatSquares. It gives you an overview of all your Squares and lets you jump straight into any content workflow.

  ![Bildschirmfoto 2026 05 29 Um 19 01 04](/images/Bildschirmfoto-2026-05-29-um-19.01.04.png)

## What you'll find here

The dashboard has two main elements:

- **Your Squares** – Each Square appears as a card showing its name, selected categories, and output format (podcast, newsletter, WhatsApp message). This lets you instantly see what each Square produces and what topics it covers.
- **A "Create new Square" button** – Start a new content stream for a different topic or output format.

## How to use it

From the dashboard, you can either:

- **Click into an existing Square** to create new content within it, or
- **Create a new Square** for a different content stream.

The dashboard is intentionally simple: see what you have, decide where to work, jump in.
