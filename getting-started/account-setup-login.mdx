## Getting an account

Accounts are created by the BeatSquares team to ensure proper access control and personalized onboarding. To request access, email us at [support@beatsquares.com](mailto:support@beatsquares.com) and we'll get you set up with Squares, podcasts, newsletters, and WhatsApp messages.

## Logging in

BeatSquares uses passwordless login. Instead of a password, you receive a one-time code by email each time you sign in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Open [app.beatsquares.com/login](https://app.beatsquares.com/login).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email (on the right-hand side)">
    Use the email address your account was created with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    You'll receive a short login code from BeatSquares within a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    Paste or type the code into the login screen to access your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Didn't receive a code?

- Check your **spam or junk folder** – the email sometimes lands there.
- Make sure you use the **exact email address** your account was set up with.
- Still nothing? Reach out to [support@beatsquares.com](mailto:support@beatsquares.com).
