## Overview

Setting up your Beatsquares account is the first step to accessing all content creation tools. This guide will walk you through creating an account, verifying your email, and logging in for the first time.

## Prerequisites

- A valid email address
- Internet connection
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Account Creation

Navigate to the Beatsquares registration page to begin account setup.

1. Go to [app.beatsquares.com/signup]
2. Fill in the registration form
3. Click "Create Account" button

**Expected Result:** You should see a confirmation message about email verification.

### Step 2: Email Verification

Check your email and verify your account to activate it.

1. Open the verification email from Beatsquares
2. Click the "Verify Email" button
3. You'll be redirected to the login page

**Screenshot/Visual:** Note where screenshots would be helpful

### Step 3: First Login

Complete your first login to access your dashboard.

1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click "Log In" button

**Expected Result:** You should be redirected to your Beatsquares dashboard.

## Verification

How users can confirm they completed the process successfully:

- You can see your dashboard with navigation menu
- Your email appears in the top-right corner
- You have access to create new content

- **Email not received:** Check spam folder or request new verification email
- **Password requirements:** Must be at least 8 characters with numbers and letters
