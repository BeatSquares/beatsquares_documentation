## What You'll Learn

In this guide, you'll learn how to:

- Create a new Square from scratch
- Configure categories and content filters
- Select output formats (Podcast, Newsletter, WhatsApp)
- Review and manage your Square settings

## Before You Start

**Prerequisites:**
- Completed account setup and login to your BeatSquares account
- Understanding of what Squares are and their purpose
- Access to the BeatSquares dashboard

**What You'll Need:**

- A clear name for your Square
- Knowledge of which content categories you want to include
- Decision on output format (Podcast, Newsletter, or WhatsApp)
- For WhatsApp: A configured WhatsApp account (set up by BeatSquares staff)

### 3. Add Categories

Each Square needs to have at least one category. As described above, the categories define the content that is available for a specific Square. _These categories determine how many articles are available when producing your Podcast, newsletter or Whatsapp messages._ To make it easy to see the impact of your choices, the "Current articles" tile shows directly how many articles match your selection.  
  
The following ways of filtering the content are:

*   Topic - general topics
    
*   Person - a person that appears in the content ingested by your organization
    
*   Location - locations in the content in your organization
    
*   Organization - organizations in your source material
    
*   Event - events found in your content
    
*   User Need - the user needs as pioneered by the BBC ([more about that here)](https://www.inma.org/blogs/newsroom-initiative/post.cfm/what-are-user-needs-and-why-should-i-care)
    
*   Data source - a specific content source associated with your organization
    
*   Publish time - the time frame from which your content should be taken
