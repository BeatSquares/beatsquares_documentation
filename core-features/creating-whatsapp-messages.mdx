---
title: "Creating WhatsApp Messages"
description: "Craft and send WhatsApp messages directly from BeatSquares for efficient communication and content distribution"
---
WhatsApp message creation in BeatSquares transforms your articles, events, or press releases into broadcast-ready content for WhatsApp channels. The workflow centers on a simple selection process: choose your source content, and BeatSquares automatically generates three message variants optimized for WhatsApp's format and audience expectations.

The system streamlines the entire publishing pipeline. Once your messages are generated, you can edit the text, add images, and choose between immediate publishing or scheduling for later. The verification system lets team members flag messages as ready-to-publish, creating a clear approval workflow when multiple people manage the same channel.

Setting up WhatsApp integration requires linking your phone through QR code scanning—similar to WhatsApp Web—and creating broadcast channels that appear in your WhatsApp app. From there, all publishing happens directly through BeatSquares' interface, with messages automatically posting to your connected channels at the times you specify. This approach keeps your content creation centralized while leveraging WhatsApp's massive reach for distribution.


## What You'll Learn

In this guide, you'll learn how to:
- Set up WhatsApp integration within BeatSquares
- Create and format WhatsApp messages
- Manage contact lists and groups
- Schedule and send messages effectively

## Before You Start

**Prerequisites:**
- Active BeatSquares account
- A mobile device with WhatsApp installed
- At least one Square created for organization
