# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about Beatsquares features, functionality, and best practices.

## Getting Started

### How do I create my first Square?
After logging in, click "Create New" on your dashboard and select "Square." Fill in the required information like name and description, then click "Create Square" to get started.

### What's the difference between Squares and other content types?
Squares are organizational containers that help you group related content together. Content types like podcasts, newsletters, and articles are the actual pieces of content you create within those Squares.

### Can I change my Square organization after creating content?
Yes, you can reorganize content between Squares and update Square information at any time through the Square settings menu.

## Core Features

### How many pieces of content can I add to one Square?
There's no limit to the number of content pieces you can add to a Square. Organize them however makes sense for your workflow and projects.

### Can I create content without associating it with a Square?
All content in Beatsquares must be associated with at least one Square for organizational purposes. You can create a general "Miscellaneous" Square for content that doesn't fit into specific projects.

### How do I edit content after I've published it?
Click on any content item in your Square to access the editing interface. Make your changes and save or republish as needed.

### Can I duplicate content across multiple Squares?
Currently, each piece of content belongs to one Square, but you can create copies of content and associate them with different Squares if needed.

## Content Creation

### What file formats are supported for uploads?
- **Images**: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- **Audio**: MP3, WAV, AAC
- **Documents**: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT
- Check specific content type documentation for detailed requirements

### Is there a limit on file sizes?
Yes, file size limits vary by content type:
- Images: 10MB maximum
- Audio files: 100MB maximum
- Documents: 25MB maximum

### How do I add formatting to my articles?
Use the rich text editor when creating articles. It supports headers, bold/italic text, links, lists, and basic formatting options.

### Can I schedule content for future publication?
Yes, most content types support scheduling. Look for the "Schedule" option when creating or editing content, and set your desired publication date and time.

## Technical Issues

### Why isn't my content saving?
This usually indicates a connection issue or browser problem. Try refreshing the page, checking your internet connection, or clearing your browser cache. See our [Common Errors](/troubleshooting/common-errors) page for detailed troubleshooting.

### My dashboard looks different than expected. What should I do?
Clear your browser cache and refresh the page. If issues persist, try using a different browser or updating your current browser to the latest version.

### How do I recover accidentally deleted content?
Contact our support team immediately at support@beatsquares.com. We may be able to recover recently deleted content, but recovery isn't guaranteed after extended periods.

## Account & Organization

### How do I change my account information?
Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select "Account Settings" to update your email, password, and other account information.

### Can I export my content from Beatsquares?
Yes, you can export your content in various formats. Look for export options in your Square settings or contact support for bulk export assistance.

### How do I delete a Square?
Go to the Square settings and select "Delete Square." Note that this will permanently delete the Square and all associated content, so export anything you want to keep first.

### Is there a way to collaborate with team members?
Team collaboration features may be available depending on your account type. Check your account settings or contact support for information about collaboration options.

## Content Strategy

### What's the best way to organize my Squares?
Organize Squares by project, theme, time period, or whatever makes sense for your workflow. Consider creating Squares for:
- Specific campaigns or projects
- Content series or ongoing themes
- Different audiences or platforms
- Time-based organization (monthly, quarterly)

### How often should I create new content?
Content frequency depends on your goals and audience. Focus on quality and consistency rather than volume. It's better to publish valuable content regularly than to overwhelm your audience.

### Can I repurpose content across different formats?
Absolutely! Beatsquares is designed to help you repurpose content. You might turn a podcast episode into newsletter content, extract key points for WhatsApp messages, or create articles based on your audio content.

## Integration & Distribution

### How do I connect external platforms?
Integration options vary by content type. Look for "Distribution" or "Integration" settings when creating content to connect with external platforms like social media, email services, or publishing platforms.

### Can I import content from other platforms?
Yes, you can manually add articles and import content from various sources. Use the "Add Manual Article" feature to bring in existing content and organize it within your Squares.

### How do I track the performance of my content?
Basic analytics are available for most content types. Look for "Analytics" or "Performance" sections within your content items to see engagement metrics and distribution statistics.

## Billing & Subscriptions

### What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
Depending on your plan, you may receive notifications about approaching limits. Upgrade your plan or contact support to discuss options for higher usage needs.

### Can I downgrade my plan?
Plan changes are typically processed at the end of your current billing cycle. Contact support to discuss downgrade options and any potential data limitations.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Account Settings > Billing to manage your subscription. You can cancel anytime, and your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

## Privacy & Security

### Is my content private and secure?
Yes, your content is stored securely and is private by default. Only you can access your content unless you specifically choose to share or publish it.

### Can I control who sees my published content?
Yes, most content types offer privacy and visibility controls. You can keep content private, share with specific people, or publish publicly depending on your needs.

### How is my data backed up?
Beatsquares automatically backs up your content. However, we recommend regularly exporting important content as an additional precaution.
